Crop Disease

Corp Disease Detection

Crop disease detection has been a crucial method of improving the yield of crops, In recent years, emerging technology has made it possible to provide detailed observation and analysis of crop leaves to enable farmers understand better the type of diseases that affect each crop. In this challenge we have taken advantage of one of these technologies to provide important details of crop disease in senegal. This was achieved by collecting datasets of crop leaves for training a deep learning model which is able to detect crop diseases by analysing the leaves. We have laid out clear plans of implementation of this model in three versions namely; interactive notebook application, Android Mobile application and USSD service for reporting.
