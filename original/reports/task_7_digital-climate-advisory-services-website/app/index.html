Improving Food Security and Crop Yield in Senegal

Objectives of this Challenge

The collaboration between GPSDD and OMDENA data scientists and machine learning engineers addresses the needs of Senegal through the application of technology on short term weather forecasts and long term climate predictions, identify plant diseases, crop prediction, crop classification, forest management, and risk assessment.
