Forest Management

Forests play an important role in agriculture and deforestation has different negative implications for climate change and yields, and the environment. As an objective we wanted to describe deforestation from 2000 to 2019 to be aware how deforestation is taking place in Senegal. Moreover we tried to build some models to predict deforestation in future years.

We explored Hansen dataset that registered forest cover change from 2001 to 2019 with the aim of describing deforestation in Senegal. Most of the areas that feature significant tree cover areas appeared to be most affected by deforestation (especially Sediouh, Fatick, Kolda, Kedougou and Tambacounda regions). Dakar area does ot have a high cover area but the tree cover area has been affected in the past years.

With the available data we tried to build some predictive models (using time series analysis, random forest and linear regression) with the aim of predicting where deforestation will take place in the future years. We did not obtain relevant results. For future investigation further data can be collected and other factors can be taken into account (firesr and logging phenomenon for instance).
