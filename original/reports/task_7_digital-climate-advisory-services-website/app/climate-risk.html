Climate Risk

Since agriculture and consequently food security in Senegal is highly dependent on the climate, it is important to understand the effects of climate on agriculture. We attempt to do that task by - first, developing models of climatic suitability for cultivation of the four key food crops for Senegal - Rice Winter, Rice CSC, Millets, Groundnut . Second, predicting drought using weather forecasts and historical data. And last but not the least, analyzing food security for each region of Senegal using climate data
